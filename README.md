# Lawyers AI SEO

AI-powered answer engine optimization and visibility for lawyers and law firms.

Lawyers AI SEO helps attorneys show up in AI search results (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, and more) by combining technical schema, content engineering, and entity authority building tailored specifically for legal services.

---

## What This Repository Is

This repo powers the AI search and SEO infrastructure behind **lawyersaiseo.com**. It contains:

- Structured content and schemas for legal services
- AI-optimized prompts and configurations
- Automation workflows for monitoring and improving AI search visibility
- Examples and templates for law firm–specific AI SEO

## Content & Schema Engineering

### Content Engineering for AI Search

- Patterns for writing legal content that:
  - Matches real user questions
  - Is factually precise and jurisdictionally scoped
  - Is easy for LLMs to summarize without distortion
- Templates for practice area pages, FAQs, and explainer content.

### Technical Schema Markup for Lawyers

- JSON‑LD schemas for:
  - `LegalService`
  - `Attorney` / `Person`
  - `Organization` (law firm)
  - `FAQPage`, `BlogPosting`, and more
- Examples of:
  - Practice‑area‑specific schemas
  - Multi‑location firm schemas
  - Attorney profile entity markup

### Entity Authority Building for Attorneys

- Structures for consistently describing:
  - Attorney names, bar numbers, jurisdictions
  - Practice areas and specialties
  - Publications, speaking, and case‑related experience
- Strategies to make attorneys and firms strong, distinct entities in AI and search knowledge graphs.
